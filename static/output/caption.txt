Pollution adds harmful or unwanted substances to our natural environment. These pollutants can be chemical, physical, or even biological. Factories and cars release smoke and gases into the atmosphere. This bad air causes breathing issues like asthma and bronchitis. Industrial waste and plastic often contaminate our rivers and oceans. Polluted water harms aquatic life and is unsafe for drinking. Heavy use of pesticides and plastic poisons the earth's surface. Damaged soil grows less food and hurts our entire ecosystem. Pollution disrupts ecosystems and creates serious risks for human health. We must protect our planet to ensure a greener future.
